rfplannr

Roadfood Trip Planner

Map-based tool to browse and filter over 1,000 diners, small-town cafes, seaside shacks, drive-ins, and BBQ joints featured in Jane and Michael Stern's Roadfood series of books.

  • Pre-filter by specifying US states of interest below
  • Zoom in on your area of interest
  • Use commands from the toolbar or keyboard to remove extraneous locations
  • Export a permalinked table view with address details to guide your trip

To get started, enter a list of one or more 2-letter state abbreviations separated by spaces or commas. (To select all states, submit a blank entry.)
